
Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup half and half
 - 1/4 cup almond milk
 - 1/2 cup olive oil
 - 2 cups TJ's cornbread mix
 - 1 egg

Directions:
  0. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  1. In a medium glass bowl beat egg, half and half, almond milk and oil.
  2. Stir in cornbread mix until integrated/moist
  3. Grease a cupcake pan and fill each cup 3/4 full
  4. Bake for 35 minutes or until poking with a fork comes back clean
  5. Allow to cool for 5 minutes
  6. Run a knife around the rim of each cake to remove
